Welcome to my online porfolio - I am a web developer qualified to work in HTML, CSS, SASS, jQuery, Javascript, Typescript, React and Redux. Having studied Graphic Design at the Royal West of England Art Academy and Bower Ashton Art and Design Department; you will find a collection of previous works featuring photography, design, illustration, photo retouching/manipulation alongside type and print. Some projects displayed here have been completed in association with the Bristol Twinning Association, European Independent Film Festival, schwet, CHAMP and Overdraft. I am capable across a range of mediums but in recent years have concentrated my efforts on photography and illustration alongside my endeavours as a developer.
